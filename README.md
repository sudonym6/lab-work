---

## **Methodology**
Each lab follows a strict documentation standard to ensure clarity and professional reporting:
1.  **Objective:** What was the goal of the lab?
2.  **Execution:** Detailed steps and the specific code/payloads used.
3.  **Proof of Concept:** Screenshots and logs demonstrating the successful exploit or finding.
4.  **Mitigation:** High-level overview of how to patch or defend against the attack.
